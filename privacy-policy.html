Privacy Policy

Collection and Use of Information

We collect your phone number and email if consent to send promotional SMS and MMS messages through Klaviyo. Your phone number will only be used for text campaigns, including promotions, updates, and offers.

Consent

By providing your phone number, you consent to receive text messages from us. Standard message and data rates may apply.

Opt-Out

You can opt-out at any time by replying "STOP" to any of our messages. This will remove your number from our text campaign list.

Data Sharing

Your phone number is shared with Klaviyo solely for the purpose of managing our text messaging campaigns. We do not sell or share your phone number with third parties for their marketing purposes.

Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your phone number and ensure compliance with privacy regulations.

For any questions regarding our text campaigns, please contact us at info@dasfilter.co.

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and stored at Das Merch.

Personal Information We Collect

  • Cookies: Data files placed on your device or computer, often including an anonymous unique identifier. For more information, visit http://www.allaboutcookies.org.
  • Log Files: Track actions occurring on the site and collect data such as IP address, browser type, Internet service provider, referring/exit pages, and date/time stamps.
  • Web Beacons, Tags, and Pixels: Electronic files used to record information about how you browse the site.
  • Order Information: Includes name, billing address, shipping address, payment information (including credit card numbers), email address, and phone number.
